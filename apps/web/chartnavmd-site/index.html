SECURITY & GOVERNANCE

Auditable AI. Human review required. Every time.

ChartNav does not route patient data through AI systems without PHI redaction, governance logging, and qualified human review. That is the baseline — not a premium feature. Designed for ophthalmology practices, hospitals, health systems, and enterprise organizations with real accountability requirements.

Full security details →
  • IBM watsonx-powered AI workflows — designed to align with watsonx.governance principles. Provider/model tracking per call.
  • Immutable AI audit log: prompt hash, output hash, model ID, PHI redaction status, security event log — append-only.
  • PHI redaction pipeline scans all 18 HIPAA Safe Harbor identifiers before any AI model call. Unredactable PHI blocks the call.
  • Prompt injection detection — hard block on token boundary attacks, jailbreak attempts, and review bypass patterns.
  • Clinician review gate: no AI output becomes final documentation without qualified human approval.
  • Role-based access — admin and clinician lead only for security console. All records org-scoped.

WHAT CHARTNAV DOES NOT CLAIM

We say what we have. Not what we don't.

Transparency about security posture is a prerequisite for trust. These are deliberate non-claims.

HIPAA Certification

ChartNav does not claim HIPAA certification. HIPAA compliance is an organizational responsibility. Practices are responsible for their BAA obligations. ChartNav is designed to support your compliance posture — not replace it.

ONC Certification

ChartNav does not hold ONC Health IT certification. Buyers requiring certified EHR technology should evaluate this requirement separately.

FedRAMP Authorization

ChartNav does not hold FedRAMP authorization. Government buyers with FedRAMP requirements should account for this in procurement evaluation.

IBM Partnership

ChartNav is not an IBM partner or IBM reseller. The use of IBM watsonx infrastructure reflects a technology integration, not a formal commercial partnership.

SOC 2

ChartNav has not completed a SOC 2 audit. SOC 2 Type II readiness is on the published security roadmap.

Clinical / Legal Advice

ChartNav does not replace legal counsel, compliance officers, or clinical judgment. Nothing in ChartNav constitutes legal, regulatory, or clinical advice.
